ABOUT US

Pokhriabong higer secondary School was established in 1989 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in hilly region of Kalimpong District. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in February.
